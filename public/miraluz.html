History

Founded in 1972, the Miraluz Industry and Madeiras Ltda. invests in technology, improvement and overcoming. Concerned about the quality of life of its employees and the communities where it operates, Miraluz grew up to its initiatives in education, citizenship and environmental management. The company is established in the city of Sengés in Parana on the border with the State of São Paulo.
The Miraluz and its employees have an important commitment to health, safety and the environment, as well as concern for the community where it operates.

Corporate vision

To be globally recognized as the most competitive Brazilian business group in business that is acting and sound economic and social image.

International Certificates

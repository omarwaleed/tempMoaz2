With more than 70 years of existence and tradition, Vimasa has always been concerned with nature and its preservation. Having its own reforestation with cutting cycle of up to 25 years, guaranteeing the self-sufficiency of raw material of excellent quality to supply its needs.

Vimasa operates with factories in the states of Santa Catarina and Paraná, been Videira factory in Santa Catarina its production unit for plywood panels.
Vimasa is present in a global way, having its products exported to more than 15 countries, which has been meeting strictly the requirements of these markets.

Panels composed of overlapping pine wood veneers, glued with WBP phenolic resin, coated with phenolic film on both faces and waterproofed on the sides with acrylic resin, ensuring a greater number of reuses.

  • Thickness (mm): 18 mm ,12mm
  • Standard Sizes (mm): 2440X1220
  • Coating: Phenolic Film 180 gm
